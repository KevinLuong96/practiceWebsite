About Cats

I know many of you are probably asking: "What is a cat?". Fear not, for after reading this section you will come to call yourself an expert on these remarkable feline companions. This section will teach you things such as their likes and dislikes, diet and so much more!

What is a cat?

A cat, or Felis felice, is a warm blooded, domesticated mammal which is common throughtout households due to their appearance, affection and love. Cats are mostly carnivorous and have a remarkable innate hunting ability. They are able to catch and eat small rodents and even birds despite some cats living their entire lives within captivity. Cats enjoy warmth, sitting in small boxes(lol) and often sit in a shape colloquially known as a "catloaf" where they tuck their arms and legs under their body to form the distinctively unusual shape.
